Privacy Policy for “Spark – Random Photo Ideas”

Effective Date: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Hangzhou Shiguangshi Technology Co., Ltd. (“the Company” or “we”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how “Spark – Random Photo Ideas” (“the App”) processes your personal information during usage.

The App adheres to the following principles:

  • Principle of minimal and necessary data collection
  • Transparency and informed consent
  • Local-first data processing
  • Compliance with international privacy regulations

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

2. Information We Collect

The core functionality of the App (random photo browsing, image processing, inspiration generation, etc.) can be used entirely offline. The App does NOT actively collect, upload, or store any of your personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Photo library content, original images, or processed images
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, IMEI, IMSI, MAC address, OAID, etc.)
    (except data collected automatically by third-party advertising SDKs disclosed in Section 4)
  • User analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash logs or performance metrics
  • Account information (no registration required)
  • Any information that directly or indirectly identifies a user

3. Necessary Online Activities

The App normally operates offline, but network access may occur in the following situations:

1. Loading Online Documents

The App connects to the internet to load the online Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. No personal data is uploaded.

2. Advertisement Display

The App loads the appropriate advertising platform depending on user region:

  • Mainland China: Tencent Youlianghui (GDT) SDK
  • Outside Mainland China: Google AdMob SDK

Outside of the above scenarios, the App does not perform any network data transmission.

4. Third-Party Advertising SDK Disclosures (Compliance Requirement)

The App does not actively collect user data. However, for ad display, anti-fraud, and attribution purposes, third-party advertising SDKs may automatically process certain necessary information. The Company does not access, store, or analyze any of this data.

5. What We Do NOT Do (Commitments)

We strictly commit that we DO NOT:

  • Upload your photos
  • Store your image data
  • Collect device unique identifiers
  • Track user behavior or build user profiles
  • Perform device fingerprinting
  • Send custom user data to advertising platforms
  • Sell any personal information

All ad-related data processing is performed solely by third-party ad platforms under their own privacy policies.

6. On-Device Image Processing & Data Security

  • All image processing is performed locally on the device
  • The App never uploads your photo library content
  • The App does not collect EXIF or location metadata
  • The App keeps no history of edited photos
  • Uninstalling the App removes all cached files automatically

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 14. Minors must use the App under guardian supervision. If we become aware of accidental collection of minor data, we will delete it immediately.
